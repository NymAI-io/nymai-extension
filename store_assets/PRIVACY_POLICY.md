# Privacy Policy for NymAI

**Last Updated:** November 2025

## 1. Introduction
NymAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the NymAI Chrome Extension (the "Extension").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1. User-Selected Content
We only collect and process content that you explicitly select for analysis. This includes:
*   **Text:** Text segments you highlight and choose to scan.
*   **Images:** Image URLs or data you right-click and choose to scan.
*   **Videos:** Video URLs or transcripts you choose to scan.

**We do NOT passively track your browsing history or collect data from websites you visit without your explicit action.**

### 2.2. Account Information
If you create an account, we collect:
*   Email address
*   Authentication tokens (via Supabase)

### 2.3. Usage Data
We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, such as:
*   Number of scans performed
*   Types of content analyzed (text vs. image)
*   Error logs

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
*   **Service Provision:** To perform the AI analysis you request (e.g., verifying text credibility or image authenticity).
*   **Improvement:** To train and improve our AI models (only if you opt-in to data sharing).
*   **Authentication:** To manage your account and subscription status.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

*   **Storage:** Your data is stored securely using Supabase and our backend infrastructure.
*   **Encryption:** All data transmitted between the Extension and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL.
*   **Retention:** We retain analysis history for your convenience, which you can clear at any time.

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to provide our functionality:
*   **Supabase:** For authentication and database services.
*   **OpenAI / Google Gemini:** For AI processing of text and images.
*   **Stripe:** For payment processing (if applicable).

These third parties adhere to strict data protection standards.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
*   Access the personal data we hold about you.
*   Request deletion of your account and data.
*   Opt-out of data collection for model training.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@nymai.io.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
